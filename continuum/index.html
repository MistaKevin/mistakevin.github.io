ANACONDA SERVER

Enterprise-ready Python foundation that powers analytic scale-up and easy operational management of software environments — on-premise or in a private cloud.
Easily deploy notebooks, software packages, and environments throughout your organization. Eliminate friction from every step in your workflow — from exploration to deployment.

ANACONDA CLUSTER

The power and ease of Anaconda spun up on your bare-metal cluster for Big Data analysis. You can also easily integrate Anaconda with your Spark or Hadoop cluster, on-premise or in the cloud.
Eliminate friction from every step in your workflow, from exploration to deployment, in your favorite big data environment.

WAKARI ENTERPRISE

Use IPython/Jupyter notebooks for data exploration, analytic script development, and collaboration connected to your enterprise identity management system with friction-less control of any software environments you need.
